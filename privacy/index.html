Privacy Policy

Data controller

Acta by Found, a brand of Found, is the data controller for personal data collected through this site. Contact: hello@actabyfound.be

Data we collect

When you use our contact form, we collect: your name, email address, and the content of your message. This data is used only to respond to your enquiry.

Purpose of processing

  • Responding to enquiries about our services
  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics to understand how visitors use this site (once analytics is configured)

Legal basis

Processing is based on your consent (contact form) and our legitimate interest (improving the service).

Retention period

Your data is kept for as long as needed to process your enquiry, and for a maximum of 3 years from our last contact.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, and object to the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, contact hello@actabyfound.be.

Cookies

This site uses Google Tag Manager for anonymous visit statistics once configured. You can decline via the consent banner. No third-party advertising cookies are used without your consent.

Processors

  • Web3Forms — delivers messages submitted through our contact form
  • Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics — anonymized visit statistics
  • GitHub Pages — site hosting
